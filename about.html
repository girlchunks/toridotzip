Hi, I'm Victoria (she/her), a transfem nonbinary photographer from Berlin.

In my work, I draw from my experience as a genderqueer person to authentically portray people how they see themselves.

I'm inspired by queer lives and communities, subcultures and human connection.

On the other hand, we denounce with righteous indignation and dislike men who are so beguiled and demoralized by the charms of pleasure of the moment, so blinded by desire
